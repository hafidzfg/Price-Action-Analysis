# Entry Type Matrix — Trend Phase → Valid Entry Types

**Purpose:** Prevent defaulting to pullback entries. Map trend phase to ALL valid Brooks entry types.

---

## 1. Trend Phase Classification

| Phase | Signature | How to Identify |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Early Trend** | Fresh breakout from TR, first legs | Breakout 3-15 bars ago. No pullback yet. Breakout bars strong (big bodies, small tails). |
| **Mid Trend** | Clean HH/HL with orderly pullbacks | 2-3 legs. Pullbacks shallow (25-40%). SOS high (14+/22). |
| **Late Trend** | Momentum slowing, overlap increasing | Bars smaller. Tails appearing. SOS dropping. Wedges forming. Pullbacks deeper (50%+). |
| **Climax** | Parabolic, 20%+ from EMA, exhaustion | 20%+ above 20-EMA. Massive bars. Gap openings. Exhaustion imminent. |
| **Transition to TR** | Failed breakouts both ways | Breakouts fail. Tails both sides. Prior trend lines broken. |
| **Trading Range** | Overlap, tails, no direction | Clear S/R. Oscillation. No HH/HL or LH/LL. |

## 2. Entry Types by Trend Phase

### EARLY TREND — Breakout Entry Priority

| Priority | Entry | Why |
|----------|-------|-----|
| **1st** | **Breakout Entry** | Enter ON the breakout bar or bar 2 of the leg. Best R:R. |
| **2nd** | **Micro-Channel Entry** | Enter on bar 2-3 of micro-channel inside the leg. |
| **3rd** | **Breakout Pullback** | Wait for first pullback to breakout level. Lower R:R but safer. |
| ❌ | Pullback to EMA | EMA hasn't caught up. Not applicable. |

### MID TREND — Multiple Valid Entries

| Priority | Entry | Why |
|----------|-------|-----|
| **1st** | **M2B/M2S (Pullback to EMA)** | High 2 / Low 2 at EMA. Highest probability in mid-trend. |
| **2nd** | **Micro-Channel Entry** | Good when pullback is only 1-2 bars (not deep enough for M2B). |
| **3rd** | **Trend Line Bounce** | Adds location to the entry. |

### LATE TREND — Caution, Favor Counter-Trend

| Priority | Entry | Why |
|----------|-------|-----|
| **1st** | **Wedge Entry (FBO)** | Enter against the 3rd push of a wedge. High probability. |
| **2nd** | **Reversal at Trend Line** | Channel/trend line break + retest. |
| 3rd | Pullback to EMA (scalp only) | Only as scalp. Trend may reverse. |
| ❌ | Swing with-trend entry | Too late. Catching the tail. |

### CLIMAX — Do Not Chase

| Priority | Entry | Why |
|----------|-------|-----|
| **1st** | **Climax Reversal / FBO** | Wait for confirmed reversal bar. |
| **2nd** | **Second Entry (L2/H2)** | Safer than fading climax directly. |
| **3rd** | **Pullback to EMA (WAIT)** | Only if trend resumes. Not actionable. |
| ❌ | Chase the spike | You'll get stopped on first pullback leg. |

### TRADING RANGE — Edge Only at Extremes

| Priority | Entry | Why |
|----------|-------|-----|
| **1st** | **Range Edge Reversal** | Buy low, sell high. Reversal bar at S/R. |
| **2nd** | **Failed Breakout (FBO)** | Trapped traders provide fuel. |
| 3rd | Nothing | Wait for breakout resolution. |

## 3. Quick Decision Table

| Trend Phase | Lead Entry | Also Consider | Never |
|-------------|-----------|---------------|-------|
| **Early Trend** | Breakout entry | Micro-channel | Wait for EMA pullback |
| **Mid Trend** | Pullback (M2B/M2S) | Micro-channel | Fade the trend |
| **Late Trend** | Wedge reversal / FBO | Channel break reversal | Swing with-trend |
| **Climax** | Climax reversal / L2 | Pullback to EMA (WAIT) | Chase spike |
| **Trading Range** | Range edge reversal | FBO | Create fake breakouts |

## 4. Anti-Bias Checklist

Before writing ANY entry recommendation, ask yourself:

- [ ] Did I classify the trend phase? Not just "trending" but specifically early/mid/late/climax?
- [ ] Did I consider at least 2 entry types OTHER than pullback-to-EMA?
- [ ] Is my recommendation based on the phase, or just habit?
- [ ] If this is an early trend, am I recommending breakout entry instead of pullback?
- [ ] If this is a climax, am I recommending reversal/FBO instead of "wait for pullback"?
